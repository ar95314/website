About us

Samyak is an international textile manufacturing organization established in the year 2007. Samyak works as a partner with all its customers and suppliers just like their internal team. With our professional approach and team work we ensure that all your international sourcing goals are accomplished with ease and grace. Our products are designed and developed keeping in mind the exact requirements of our clients thereby, offering complete customer satisfaction.

We have highly dedicated and knowledgeable professionals who constantly monitor the markets to identify best materials so that we are able to deliver the best assortment. Our entire team is divided into as per their respective departments, which facilitate the commercial aspect of work and offer the best solutions to the clients. Moreover, our diligence, dedication, and firm commitment to quality have made possible for us to make our presence valuable in the textile industry today.

Our reputation in the market is based on the consistency of our quality and richness of variety. We are particularly proud on our 100% track record of on time delivery of requisite fabric. We had entered into exclusive tie-up with multiple spinning units for an uninterrupted supply of yarn which facilitates smooth delivery chain. Our lead time in general is much better than the industry averages..

Our Company at present focusing on increasing presence in countries like Afghanistan Dubai, Libya, Egypt, Yemen, Nigeria, South Africa, Mexico, Panama, Vietnam.
We are one of the Acclaimed Manufacturers and Exporters of Poly-Viscose, Poly-Wool, 100% Wool, 100 % Cotton and Poly – Cotton Fabrics..
